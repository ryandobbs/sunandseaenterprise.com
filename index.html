Alarm AI

The alarm clock that checks conditions before it wakes you. Set a time and your conditions — no rain, offshore wind, fresh snowfall, swell height — and Alarm AI checks the forecast overnight. Conditions met, it fires. Conditions missed, you sleep in. If it can't check, it always fires: you're never left sleeping.

Built for dawn patrol surf checks, powder-day starts, morning runs, and any routine the weather gets a vote in. No account, no ads, no tracking.
